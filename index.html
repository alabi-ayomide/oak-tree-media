OUR APPROACH

Our team works as media career development consultants. Our sole focus is on what we call ‘future famous’ actors, writers, directors, DPs, and producers. We help our clients raise name recognition and funding using a unique PR and promotions approach. Although our way of working is highly non-standard… so is our success metric in getting quality projects and people seen by top tier Industry professionals.

Our little incubator in the hills above LA has so far helped shelter and nurture three Academy Awards, two Sundance, and numerous international film festival awards (and counting). The actors we helped launch over the years have gone on to have speaking roles in over 300 Feature Films and TV Shows. Some are now major A-list stars… and others are full-time supporting actors. In addition, some of the top grossing films and film franchises of the past thirty years were first brainstormed by then-unknown newcomers under our roof.

***

Client Requirements-

In our opinion, the potential new client must have honest future-famous potential. We will then take the applicant through a structured evaluation process of all relevant materials. This will include a review of that client's primary written objectives... and their answers to a detailed Client Questions Memo. In addition, the applicant must be at least modestly funded to be able to cover the initial 30-day evaluation period.

CLIENT SUCCESS

We help our clients make their own succes. This starts by helping them undertanding the business of the 'The Business'... then building strong and effective forward facing outreach and promotional materials. For success, one must drop any hint of false ego, fantasy tripping, and stop being impressed by namedroppers. We guide our clients to begin thinking and working logically.

Good acting and successful auditioning (ie, getting the job and making money) are two entirely and distinct skill sets. Good actors… who don’t win auditions… lose the game. There is almost never any such thing as being 'discovered'.

Being a good writer… and actually selling one’s intellectual property… takes two entirely different mindsets. Great writers… who don’t sell their projects… go hungry. Your friends and your mom may think your work is wonderful... but if no one else ever sees the script or book, no one else will ever care.

Ambitous new producers and talented new directors… with no funding and no real Industry access… will never be seen. They sit at home hoping and waiting for a White Knght with Big Bucks to come along and give them money. This is not reality... this is a total loser game.
